The general theme of my four lectures will be writing functional programs and proving them correct in Coq.

Suggested Reading: If you have time, please read or skim the suggested reading before the lectures.

The Coq files are compatible with Coq 8.4pl3. The problems are listed in approximate order of increasing difficulty. Coq novices should start at the beginning; those with more experience can skip to whichever problem interests them the most.

Some of these Coq files import SfLib.v or Imp.v from Software Foundations.
